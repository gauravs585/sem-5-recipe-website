Pav Bhaji

Ingredients:

For the Bhaji (Vegetable Curry):

- 2 cups mixed vegetables (e.g., potatoes, peas, carrots, bell peppers)

- 1 large onion, finely chopped

- 2 tomatoes, chopped

- 2-3 cloves of garlic, minced

- 1-inch piece of ginger, grated

- 2 tablespoons Pav Bhaji masala (readily available spice blend)

- Salt, to taste

- Butter or oil for cooking

- Chopped fresh coriander leaves for garnish

For the Pav (Buns):

- 4-6 pav buns or dinner rolls

- Butter for toasting

Instructions:

1. Boil the mixed vegetables until they are soft, then mash them coarsely.

2. In a large pan, heat some butter or oil. Add minced garlic, grated ginger, and chopped onions. Saut√© until the onions become translucent.

3. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and the oil starts to separate.

4. Add the Pav Bhaji masala and salt. Mix well and cook for a few minutes.

5. Stir in the mashed vegetables and continue cooking, mashing and stirring until everything is well combined.

6. If the mixture is too dry, add a little water to reach your desired consistency. Simmer for a few more minutes.

7. While the bhaji is simmering, slice the pav buns in half and toast them with butter on a griddle or in a pan until they're lightly browned.

8. Serve the Pav Bhaji hot with a dollop of butter, garnished with fresh coriander leaves, and the toasted pav buns onthe side.

Enjoy your homemade Pav Bhaji, a flavorful and hearty Indian street food!
