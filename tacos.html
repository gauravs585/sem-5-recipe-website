Tacos

Ingredients:

For the Taco Filling:

- 1 pound ground beef (or ground chicken, turkey, or a meat substitute)

- 1 packet of taco seasoning mix (or use homemade taco seasoning)

- 1/2 cup diced onions

- 1/2 cup diced tomatoes

- 1/2 cup shredded lettuce

- 1/2 cup grated cheddar or Monterey Jack cheese

- 1/4 cup sour cream

- 1/4 cup salsa

- 8 small taco shells or tortillas

Instructions:

1. In a skillet, cook the ground meat over medium-high heat until it's browned and fully cooked, breaking it into smaller pieces as it cooks.

2. Drain any excess fat from the cooked meat and return it to the skillet.

3. Add the diced onions and taco seasoning mix to the cooked meat. Follow the instructions on the seasoning packet for the right amount of water or simply add about 1/2 cup of water. Stir well and simmer for a few minutes until the mixture thickens and the flavors meld together.

4. While the taco filling is simmering, warm the taco shells or tortillas according to the package instructions, or you can heat them in a dry skillet for a few seconds on each side.

5. To assemble the tacos, place a spoonful of the seasoned meat mixture into each taco shell.

6. Top the meat with diced tomatoes, shredded lettuce, grated cheese, sour cream, and salsa, or any other toppings you prefer.

7. Serve the tacos immediately while they are hot and enjoy!
