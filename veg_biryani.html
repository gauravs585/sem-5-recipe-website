Veg Biryani

Ingredients:

- 1 cup Basmati rice

- 2 cups mixed vegetables (e.g., carrots, peas, beans)

- 1 large onion, thinly sliced

- 2 tomatoes, chopped

- 2 cloves of garlic, minced

- 1-inch piece of ginger, grated

- 2 tablespoons biryani masala

- 1/2 cup plain yogurt

- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil or ghee (clarified butter)

- Salt, to taste

- Fresh coriander leaves and fried onions for garnish (optional)

Instructions:

1. Wash the Basmati rice and soak it in water for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.

2. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil or ghee over medium heat. Add the sliced onions and sauté until they turn golden brown and crispy. Remove some for garnish, if desired.

3. Add the minced garlic and grated ginger to the pot and sauté for a minute or until the raw smell disappears.

4. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they soften and the oil starts to separate.

5. Stir in the biryani masala and cook for a few minutes.

6. Add the mixed vegetables and sauté briefly.

7. Mix in the yogurt and salt, and cook for a couple of minutes.

8. Add the drained rice and stir to combine with the vegetables and spices.

9. Pour in 2 cups of water and bring the mixture to a boil.

10. Once it starts boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the rice is cooked and the liquid is absorbed. You can also use a rice cooker for this step.

11. Once the biryani is ready, fluff the rice with a fork and garnish with fresh coriander leaves and fried onions, if desired.

12. Serve your vegetable biryani hot, either on its own or with raita (yogurt with spices) on the side.

Enjoy your homemade vegetable biryani!
