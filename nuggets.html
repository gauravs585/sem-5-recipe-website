Cheese Nuggets

Here's a short and simple recipe for making cheese nuggets:

Ingredients:

- 1 cup of cheese cubes (cheddar, mozzarella, or your preferred cheese)

- 1 cup breadcrumbs

- 1/2 cup all-purpose flour

- 2 eggs, beaten

- 1/2 teaspoon salt

- 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

- Oil for frying

Instructions:

1. Start by preparing a breading station. In one bowl, place the all-purpose flour. In another bowl, add the beaten eggs. In a third bowl, combine the breadcrumbs with salt and black pepper.

2. Take a cheese cube and dip it into the flour, making sure it's coated evenly.

3. Next, dip the floured cheese cube into the beaten eggs, ensuring it's well-covered.

4. Roll the cheese cube in the breadcrumb mixture, pressing gently to help the breadcrumbs adhere.

5. Repeat steps 2 to 4 for a second coating by dipping the cheese cube back into the egg and breadcrumbs. This double coating ensures the cheese stays inside when frying.

6. Place the breaded cheese nugget on a tray or plate and repeat the process for the remaining cheese cubes.

7. Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat to about 350°F (175°C).

8. Carefully add a few cheese nuggets to the hot oil and fry them until they turn golden brown, which should only take a minute or two.

9. Using a slotted spoon, remove the cheese nuggets from the oil and place them on a plate lined with paper towels to drain any excess oil.

10. Serve the cheese nuggets hot, perhaps with a dipping sauce like ketchup, ranch dressing, or marinara sauce.

These cheese nuggets make a delightful and cheesy snack or appetizer. They're sure to be a hit with cheese lovers of all ages.
