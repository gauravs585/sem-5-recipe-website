Gulab Jamun

Ingredients:

For the Gulab Jamun:

- 1 cup of Gulab Jamun mix (readily available in stores)

- A few tablespoons of milk (as needed)

For the Sugar Syrup:

- 1 1/2 cups of sugar

- 1 cup of water

- A few drops of rose water (optional)

Instructions:

1. Make the sugar syrup by combining sugar and water in a saucepan. Bring it to a boil and simmer for 5-7 minutes until it reaches a one-string consistency. Add a few drops of rose water if you like and set it aside.

2. In a mixing bowl, add the Gulab Jamun mix.

3. Gradually add a few tablespoons of milk to the mix and knead it into a soft dough. Be careful not to make it too sticky or too dry. The dough should be smooth and pliable.

4. Divide the dough into small, even-sized portions and roll them into smooth balls. Ensure there are no cracks on the surface. Keep them covered to prevent drying out.

5. Heat ghee or oil in a deep frying pan over medium heat. The temperature should be around 350°F (175°C).

6. Carefully slide a few dough balls into the hot oil and fry them until they turn golden brown. Make sure to maintain the oil at a consistent temperature to ensure even cooking.

7. Once the Gulab Jamun balls are golden brown, remove them from the oil and drain on a paper towel.

8. While they are still warm, place the fried Gulab Jamun into the prepared sugar syrup. Allow them to soak in the syrup for a few hours or until they become soft and spongy.

9. Serve Gulab Jamun warm or at room temperature. They are a delightful and sweet treat enjoyed on various occasions.

This simplified recipe uses Gulab Jamun mix, which is readily available in stores and simplifies the preparation process. Enjoy your Gulab Jamun!
