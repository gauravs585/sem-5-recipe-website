Pasta

Ingredients:

- 8 ounces (about 2 cups) of pasta (e.g., spaghetti, penne, or your choice)

- Water for boiling

- Salt

- Sauce of your choice (e.g., marinara, Alfredo, or pesto)

Instructions:

1. Boil a large pot of water.

2. Add salt and pasta to boiling water.

3. Cook pasta according to package instructions (usually 8-12 minutes).

4. Drain the cooked pasta.

5. Toss with your preferred sauce.

6. Serve and enjoy!

You can also add grated Parmesan cheese or fresh herbs for extra flavor.
